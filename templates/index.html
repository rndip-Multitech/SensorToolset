Welcome

This toolkit helps you monitor uplinks, schedule downlinks, and manage payload decoders.

Step 1: See your first uplink
Open Uplinks, connect to your gateway MQTT broker (often 127.0.0.1, topic lora/+/up), and confirm messages are arriving.
Step 2: Decode to human-readable data
Pick Auto or a specific decoder. Use Decoded / Raw / Both view to compare results. If needed, upload a custom decoder on Decoders.
Step 3: Confirm the device is healthy
Open Sensors to see last update, status badges, and RSSI/SNR health. Use date ranges (1h/24h/7d/30d) to review history.

Common mistakes (and fixes)

  • No messages: verify broker/port/topic and that devices are joined; try topic lora/+/up.
  • Decoded looks wrong: switch to Raw view to confirm payload, then select the correct decoder (or Auto) per device.
  • Device shows “Offline”: check RSSI/SNR on Sensors; move the device/gateway or improve antenna placement.
  • Downlink not working: confirm DevEUI selection and use a sensor-specific encoder (upload in Decoders if needed).

Uplinks

Connect to your gateway’s LoRa Network Server MQTT broker and view incoming uplinks.

  • Default: broker 127.0.0.1, topic lora/+/up
  • View modes: Decoded / Raw / Both
  • Import: Upload a JSON history from the gateway to merge packets into the cache (table, filters, decode, export)
  • Export: CSV/JSON from browser cache
